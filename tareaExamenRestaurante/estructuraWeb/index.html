SINCE 1975

We are a close-knit family with a rich history, proudly running this restaurant since 1975. For over a century, our family has worked side by side, passing down recipes, traditions, and a love for great food from one generation to the next. Our commitment to quality and hospitality has remained constant, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere that feels like home to our customers.

We take pride in our long-standing heritage, and every dish we serve carries a piece of our family’s story.
